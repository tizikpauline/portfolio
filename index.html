About

Hey! I’m Polina Tizik and I am a graphic and UI/UX designer from Moscow. At the moment I work at design company AIC as UI/UX designer and study product and digital design in HSE Art and Design School. For 1 year I worked at the multimedia interactive studio Sila Sveta as graphic designer and concept artist.

I design apps and websites as well as create identity, branding design and art concepts. I adore to photograph, write music in Ableton and create artworks in TouchDesigner.
